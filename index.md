## About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at Jagiellonian University Medical College working at the intersection of digital health, wearable technologies, and biostatistics. My research focuses on wearable-derived digital phenotyping, particularly the characterization of sleep, physical activity, and rest–activity rhythms and their associations with mental health outcomes.

## Research Interests

- **Digital health:** remote patient monitoring, digital therapeutics, digital biomarkers, digital phenotyping
- **Wearable devices:** actigraphy, sleep monitoring, physical activity tracking, passive sensing
- **Biostatistics:** longitudinal data analysis, mixed-effects models, missing data methods, clustering, explanatory modeling, causal inference
- **Electronic Health Records:** patient pathways, clinical phenotyping, healthcare utilization patterns
